Biography

Early Life

Jeremy was born in Ohio. This was not a terribly influential experience for him because he only lived there for a few years. He soon moved to a suburb in the greater Boston area where, aside from a few excursions, he would remain for the rest of his life.

Middle Years

Jeremy's middle years, in middle and high school, were fairly mundane. The one exception to this was the year that he spent in New Zealand during 8th grade. This was a truly wonderful experience and he would recommend traveling there to basically anyone. Jeremy also picked up the clarinet during this period and participated in concert and marching band.

Now and the Future

At some point during senior year of high school, Jeremy managed to apply to and get accepted to Tufts University where he no attends. This has been a very good experience for Jeremy. He discovered a new passion in ballroom dance and solidified his interest in Computer Science. Looking towards the future, Jeremy is excited to keep on learning!
